# PromptsRadar - Your Radar for Trending AI Prompts

![PromptsRadar Logo](https://promptsradar.com/_next/image?url=%2Fassets%2Fimages%2Flogo-title.png&w=256&q=75) 

## Description

PromptsRadar is a web platform built to help users discover and share trending AI prompts for tools like ChatGPT, MidJourney, and more. It boosts creativity, productivity, and design by providing curated, daily-updated prompts across various categories. Users can search for prompts, submit their own, and subscribe to a weekly newsletter for the top 10 trending prompts.

Whether you're a designer, developer, student, or AI enthusiast, PromptsRadar provides ready-to-use prompts to enhance your workflow and spark innovation.

## Features

- **Search Functionality**: Easily search for prompts using keywords (e.g., via `/prompts?search=` endpoint).
- **Daily Curated Updates**: Fresh, trending prompts added every day.
- **Prompt Submission**: Share your own powerful AI prompts with the community.
- **Newsletter Subscription**: Receive 10 trending prompts every Friday directly in your inbox (no spam, easy unsubscribe).
- **Battle Prompts**: Explore competitive or comparative prompt battles in a dedicated section.
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and session management powered by Next-Auth.

## Categories

Prompts are organized into intuitive categories to help users find what they need quickly. Here are the main categories available on the platform:

- **Logo Design**: Ready-to-use prompts for creating unique and bold logos.
- **Character Design**: Ideas for generating characters in various styles and themes.
- **Product Mockups**: Prompts to design and showcase products effectively.
- **Interior & Architecture**: Visualization prompts for interiors and architectural concepts.
- **Fashion & Apparel**: Design prompts for clothing, fashion styles, and apparel.
- **Photography & Portraits**: Creative prompts for generating photography and portraits.
- **Agents & RAG**: Prompts related to Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI agents, and Agentic RAG.
- **Programming and Coding**: Assistance for developers with coding and programming tasks.
- **Educational Assistance and Homework**: Prompts tailored for students and educational purposes.

For users browsing the site:
- Visit [https://promptsradar.com/](https://promptsradar.com/) to explore prompts.
- Submit a prompt via the submission form on the site.
- Subscribe to the newsletter for weekly updates.
